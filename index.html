Enjoy culture and sports in this vibrant

Top Three Activities to Do at Torshov

Attend a Show or Concert

The Torshov Theater is a great place to spend your night out.

Go for a Picnic in the Local Park

The Torshov Park is a perfect place for a picnic, getting some tan, or just chilling out.

Play Sports at the Lilleborg Field

Play volleyball, football, basketball, or lift some weights.
